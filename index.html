About Cannabidiol

The CBD Oil

Cannabidiol, or CBD, is a chemical compound found in the Cannabis sativa plant. Unlike THC, also found in the same plant, CBD is not psychoactive and it doesn’t get you high.

CBD has multiple health benefits such as reducing anxiety and depression, helping with insomnia, being a pain reliever and helping with inflammation and speed up recovery time, lowers high blood pressure, it can reduce seizure disorders.
